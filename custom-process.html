CUSTOM
PROCESS

Our custom cycle kit service has developped through our customers' early recognition of our quality, and their desire to have bespoke designed and manufactured Milltag branded kit. We now pride ourselves as being the best quality custom jerseys on the market, with the highest customer service and a keen eye for great design. We believe your custom jerseys shoud be worth wearing more than any off-the-shelf kit and therefore work tirelessly to provide you with uniquely designed and supremely finished products.

1.CONTACT US

We treat every project on a completely individual basis, so it’s best to talk to us about your requirements, particularly if you have a specific deadline for kit delivery. Click here to send a message direct from our site or email hello@milltag.cc

2.SAMPLES

We understand that you may not have seen a Milltag product in the flesh and want to check fit and finish before you place an order. Contact us either by email to hello@milltag.cc detailing your requirements or call us on 0207 138 3592 and we can arrange to send samples to you. We request a £50 deposit for sending out samples which is fully refundable when returned.

3.DESIGN

With over 30 years experience in graphic and branding design we know how to design the best looking cycle kit. No other custom operators offer the opportunity to have your kit designed from scratch like a pro team with our level of design expertise and brand sensitivity. We don’t just use design templates, re-colour them and slap your logo on, we consider how it would create an entire range. That’s why we like to call it Milltag Bespoke. You may have a design that doesn’t need changing and that’s fine - we understand the importance of tradition and continuity. But if you want an update to your club kit we can help you create a design that members will love for years to come. We even consider how our logo fits with your kit. We’ll find the best position that works in harmony with the design and even change its colour to match. After all, this is your kit. A full bespoke design of a kit from scratch starts at £250 for jersey, bibs and cap and then £50 per item that follows. These are one-off costs to cover design time and artwork set-up. However, we deal with each customer on an individual basis and you may just require an small update to a current design, so please contact us to discuss your needs.

4.ARTWORK

To create the sharpest print, you should provide your logos and graphics in either Adobe Illustrator ‘.ai’ or Encapsulated Post Script ‘.eps’ formats. We can also print high resolution photographs but these need to be large scale and over 300 dpi. Artwork colours should be either Pantone or CMYK colours. There is no extra charge for number of colours.

5.ORDERING

We always request that you order at least ten items of a single product. However you can order less and pay the following surcharges: 3-5 at 25% and 6-9 at 10%.
If you are a larger cycle club, the Milltag Club+ system provides a secure online store for you to login during a requested order period and purchase the items they require from us direct. As well as removing a major headache of collecting orders and money from your members. Once the total order is collated and approved at the end of the period, production commences. Dispatch is then around 6 weeks to a club representative for distribution.

6.PRODUCTION

Even during our busiest period (January to July), we will deliver to you in under 8 weeks from ordering, often closer to 6 weeks. Outside this period it’s nearer 4-6 weeks. If you have a specific deadline please let us know as soon as possible so we can check our production schedule and see what we can do. The earlier you can tell us the better so we can book you a production slot - artwork can always follow later.

7.DELIVERY

We ship all products worldwide but obviously costs vary depending on the size of the order, so contact us to discuss.

8.PAYMENT

Payment is 50% up front and rest on delivery for your first order. After that it is we require full payment 45 days from the order date.
